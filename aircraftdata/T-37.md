# T-37 Tweet and A-37 Dragonfly

## Versions and Variants

### T-37C

- [ADC](T-37C.json)

ADC from TSOH, as a variant of the A-37B. There it is referred to as the "AT-37", but I believe the correct designation is "T-37C", which is the variant of the T-37 with two weapons stations.

### A-37B

- [ADC](A-37B.json)

ADC from TSOH.

### OA-37B

- [ADC](OA-37B.json)

ADC from TSOH, as a variant of the AT-37.

## Operation History

T-37 introduced in 1957.

AT-37 introduced in 1967.
