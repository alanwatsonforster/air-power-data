# Wasp

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Westland_Wasp

HAS.1

Almost identical to the Scout

Pintle-mounted door GPMG (LSD or RSD) (see Wasp)
- Four SS.11 or two AS.12
- Two Mk. 44 or one Mk.46 torpedo, two Mk 11 depth charges, or one WE.177 nuclear depth bomb

RN service from 1964 to 1988. Also served with the Royal Malaysian Navy from 1988 to 1998, RNZN from 1966 to 1998, Royal Netherlands Navy from 1966 to 1981, the Indonesian Navy from about 1980 to 1998, the Brazilian Navy from 1966, and the South African Navy from 1963 to 1990.
