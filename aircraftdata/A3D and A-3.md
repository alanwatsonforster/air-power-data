# A3D and A-3 Skywarrior

## Versions and Variants

### A3D-2/A-3B (Early)

- [ADC](A3D-2.json)/[ADC](A-3B.json)

ADC from TSOH.

Early version with tail guns.

The SAC states that the tail guns are two 20 mm M3 each with 500 rounds. Added in [deb3d24](https://github.com/alanwatsonforster/glass/commit/deb3d241a568d98687c6c7ecaf3e1aa43b0a0722).

### A3D-2/A-3B (Late)

- [ADC](A3D-2.json)/[ADC](A-3B.json)

ADC from TSOH.

Late version without tail guns.

### A3D-2Q/EA-3B (Late)

- [ADC](A3D-2Q.json)/[ADC](EA-3B.json)

ADC from TSOH, as a variant of the A-3.

## Operational History

Introduced March 1956.
