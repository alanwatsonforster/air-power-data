## Notes and Changes

### Leading-Edge Slats

All ADCs are modified with leading-edge slats from an ADC supplied by Carl Smeaton.

Drendel (pp. 4-5) describes the slats the F-5A/B and states that the F-5A slats extended below 300 mph.

Drendel (p. 39) describes the slats on the NF-5A.

Drendel (pp. 44) describes the slats on the F-5E (or more precisely the F-5A-21 prototype that became the F-5E).
