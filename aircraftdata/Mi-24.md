# Mil Mi-24

## Mi-24 (Hind-A)

- 1973
- Four 55 mm RPs plus four Swatter RGs
- 12.7 mm Afanasev A-12.7 gun in nose
  - 800-1100 RPM
- Poor field of view


## Mi-24D (Hind-D)

- 1973
- 12.7 mm Yak-B gun
  - Four-barrel gatling
  - 4000-5000 rpm
  - 1470 rounds = 10 shots at 4500 rpm
  - +/- 60 deg in azimuth
- Better field of view
- Afghanistan: RWR, flares, exhaust cooling
- Falanga-P
- FLIR

## Hi-24V (Hind-E)
- 1976
- Like -24D
- Spiral
- Late model had CM upgrade like Hind-D


## Hind-24P (Hind-F)
- 1981
- Like V
- Fixed 30 mm GShK-30K
  - 750 rounds (Goebel)
  - 300 (low) to 2000-2600 (high)
  - Uses 30 x 165 mm shell with muzzle velocity of 3080 ft/s
  - 750 at 2300 RPM = 10 shots
  - GAU-8 has 3900 RPM, uses a 30 x 173 mm shell, and has a muzzle velocity of 3324 ft/s. Rating is 7/12_.
  - About half as good as the GAU-8.
  - MiG-27F has GSh-30-2 with rating of 6/8_.
- Presumably all had the CM upgrade, as the -24P was developed on the basis of experience in Afghanistan (Goebel)
