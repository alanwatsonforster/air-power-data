### Load Limits

Kettle has:

- Four 500 lb, two 1000 lb, one 2000 lb BB or one 1300L FT on central station
  - Load limit for station 3 must be:
    - 2000 BB + 200 for DRs = 2300
    - 2450 for 1300L FT
- Two 500 lb or one 1000 lb or one 1300L/1700L FT on station 2/4
- One Magic or Shafrir on station 1/5

- APJ 39 has Mirage IIIS:
  - Limit of 2550 for centerline
  - Inner wings with limit of 1230 but can be overloaded to 3340 and carry 1300L or 1700L FT with max turn rates of HT or TT
  - 1300L FT with weight = 2540, load = 4.0/3.0, and fuel = 108
  - 1700L FT with weight = 3340, load = 5.0/3.5, and fuel = 142

With two empty 1700L FTs and two Magics load is 9 = DT
