## Mi-28N Havoc-B

- 2010 service in Russia
- single-barrelled 30 mm 2A42 gun
- 300 rounds. 300 RPM air-to-ground and 900 RPM air-to-air
- 30 shots
- Four pylons. 
  - Each can carry RP 
  - Each can carry 8 x AT-6 Spiral-2 (Ataka) SACLOS
  - Two 23 mm UPK-23-250 gun pods each containing a GSh-23L with 250 rounds
- Exhaust suppressors
- Chaff/Flare on wing tips
- VAS + FLIR
- Mast-mounted Kinzal-V or Arbalet radar
