# SNCASE SE.535 Mistral

## ADCs

- [Mistral](Mistral.json)

## Notes and Changes

### Original ADC

The original ADC is from Chris Perleberg's collection of updated ADCs.

### Changes

The original ADC has a CC of 1.0 for 1/2 configuration at MH altitude but 0.5 for CL at the same altitude. I have used 0.5 for both.

### Ejector Seat

Fitted with SNCASO ejector seat (Watkins)

## Service

### Mistral

- In service May 1953 (Watkins) until at least 1961 (Watkins)
- Saw combat in Tunisia and Algeria (Watkins)
